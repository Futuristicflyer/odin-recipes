Sloppy Joes

Description

This Sloppy Joe recipe has a great flavor. Not too sweet but has a great mix and tastes amazing!

Ingredients

  • 1 small onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • hamburger buns

Steps

  1. Dice small onion and garlic clove to a reasonable size and put into a bowl.
  2. Add ketchup, worcestershire sauce, mustard, and brown sugar into a separate bowl.
  3. Add light salt to your ground beef and cook until it is a nice golden brown and then add in the sauce and onions/garlic into the pan.
  4. After a couple of minutes of mixing grab your hamburger buns and place ground beef inbetween buns and serve ready to eat!
